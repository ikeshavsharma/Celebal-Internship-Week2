# Brief Business Insights

## Dataset Overview
- Total Records: 9,994
- Total Customers: 793
- Total Products: 1,862

## Sales Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales revenue (~836K).
- Furniture generated strong sales but comparatively low profit.
- Office Supplies delivered strong profitability despite lower revenue.

## Product Insights
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the highest revenue and profit generating product.
- Several printer products generated negative profits and require review.

## Customer Insights
- Sean Miller was the highest revenue generating customer.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of customers.

## Regional Insights
- California was the top performing state in terms of revenue and profit.
- Texas generated the highest overall loss despite strong sales volume.

## Discount Insights
- Higher discount levels were associated with lower profitability.
- Discounts above 60% frequently resulted in negative average profit.

## Data Quality
- No missing Sales values.
- No missing Customer IDs.
- Only one missing Profit value was identified.
